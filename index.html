About Me

I have always found myself at the confluence of creativity and technology. From the recording industry, to technical sales, to IT helpdesk, my career has taken me in a few turns before discovering software development. I’ve learned that I am happiest when I’m using both sides of my brain. Merging the logical flow of an algorithm with an attractive, intuitive interface hits a sweet spot - and it’s the reason I love and am pursuing a career in full-stack web development.

When I was eight my parents found me with a screwdriver, with our VCR in fourteen pieces on the living room floor. I’ve always been driven to understand how and why things work, and this has evolved into a natural skill for troubleshooting, debugging, and problem solving.

My long customer service and sales background makes me the rare geek with soft skills. I have a lot of experience breaking down technical concepts in layman’s terms. I can talk with clients and not only understand their requirements, but build a relationship and make them FEEL understood.
